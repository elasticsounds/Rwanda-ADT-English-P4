Post Office Box 69

Magerwa, Rwanda

 

Dear Joselyn,

 

I hope you are very fine. I am also fine. I am writing to explain to you the wonderful things you missed when we visited the museum. We went with our parents and other friends during the weekend.

We saw and learnt amazing things at the museum. We saw a big map of Rwanda as we entered the museum. We learnt of how people lived in the past. For example, we saw pots in different sizes, which they used for storing milk and water.

We also saw the different houses people lived in. They used to build huts. The huts built for kings were very special. We entered one of the king’s huts too. We saw different dressing styles and haircuts. The styles depended on one’s role in society.
