Let’s always turn the water off whenever we are not using it. Do not let rain water be wasted. Store it for future use.

We must reduce rubbish. Let’s reduce the rubbish like, bottles, cans, papers by reusing, or recycling them so that they can be remade into new products. Don’t buy new things you won’t use and don’t use polythene papers as packaging.

Use less packaging. We must cut down fewer trees. Let’s save some trees in our areas to work as wind breakers and give us shade. If possible, don’t cut down any trees.

We must plant more trees. Let’s always plant trees in our schools, communities and compounds. Whenever we cut one tree, let’s plant two more trees. We must burn less wood. Let’s reduce on the use of charcoal for cooking. We can use biogas for cooking and solar power.

Change starts now. We must go and tell everyone about it. Let’s use attractive posters, give presentations that explain how people’s actions can cause and reduce climate changes.
