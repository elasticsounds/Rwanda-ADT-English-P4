5.6 Telling about helping in the community

My name is Ishema. I like the last Saturdays of the month very much. Last Saturday was the last Saturday of the month.

 

I prepared myself and got ready to participate in Umuganda, also known as community work. In our village, our chief went around collecting everyone to clean up a certain field which would be used as a football pitch.

 

We dug up and slashed the tall grass. My parents went with the rest of the village members to clear up the garbage area. They loaded it on to big rubbish truck and burnt some of it. We were happy helping clean up our community area.
