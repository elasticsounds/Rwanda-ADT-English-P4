4.2 Telling about weather in the past

  1. What was the weather like last week?

  Ans. Last week it was rainy.

  1. What was the weather like yesterday?

  Ans. Yesterday, it was cool.

  1. What was the weather like last month?

  Ans. Last month it was mostly dry.

  1. What was the weather like last year?

  Ans. Last year, it was wet.

Can you try to remember what the weather was like last week, or yesterday?
