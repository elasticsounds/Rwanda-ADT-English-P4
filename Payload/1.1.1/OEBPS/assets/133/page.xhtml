6.4 Telling about animals in Rwanda

The wildlife of Rwanda has got a lot of plants and animals. It has got natural vegetation and three major National parks. The three protected areas are Akagera National Park, Volcanoes National Park and Nyungwe Forest National Park.

Akagera National Park covers an area of 108,500 ha. It is located in the North Eastern side of Rwanda. It has so many kinds of animals. There are some bird species, buffalos, zebras, giraffes, antelopes, hippopotamuses, elephants, warthogs, a few Lions, gazelles, crocodiles, snakes and rare giant pangolins.

Another national park is the Nyungwe Forest national park. This covers an area of 101,900 ha. It is found in the South Western part of Rwanda. It has many kinds of animals.
