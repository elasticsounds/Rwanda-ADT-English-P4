Rights

Responsibilities

We have a right to be safe. This means that we have a right to protection from harm and discrimination, to be treated

with respect and have at least five adults who love us

Our responsibility is to know who to

ask help from and follow safety rules.

We have a right to health and shelter. This means that we are free to play and receive the best health care.

Our responsibility is to play safely, keep our community, home and school clean and have a good sense about what to eat.

We have a right to be heard while

expressing our opinions. This means we should be listened to and be taken seriously when we ask and receive help.

Our responsibility is to honor our commitments and to respect the opinions of others.

 

We have a right to fair treatment.

This means being treated without

any biases.

 

Our responsibility is to treat others fairly and equally.

 

We have a right to good education.

This means being given the education we need to have a bright future.

 

Our responsibility is to take care of our school material and do our best at school.
